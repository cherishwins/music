## 4. SONIC ARCHITECTURE

### Wiz Khalifa "Cloud Rap" DNA
- Atmospheric high-frequency synth pads (wide stereo)
- Melodic minimalist trap drums
- Heavy reverb/delay on vocals ("floating" sensation)
- BPM: 140-150 (half-time feel at 70-75)
- **Tags**: `[cloud rap]`, `[atmospheric synthesizer]`, `[reverb heavy vocals]`, `[major to minor progression]`

### Travis Scott "Psychedelic Trap" Layer
- Bit-crushed 808s with glide
- Heavily autotuned ad-libs/screams
- Dynamic shifts, fake drops
- Darker tone, minor keys, dissonance
- **Tags**: `[distorted 808]`, `[bitcrusher]`, `[psychedelic texture]`
- **Negative**: `[clean bass]`, `[acoustic drums]`

### Squid Game Tension Layer (Bridge Secret Weapon)
- Recorder flute or children's percussion
- Juxtaposition of innocent sounds with brutal reality
- Subconsciously triggers "Red Light, Green Light" anxiety
- **Tags**: `[recorder flute]`, `[children's percussion]`, `[grotesque contrast]`
